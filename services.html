Services

Included Services:

  • In home pet sitting (dogs, cats, rodents, reptiles, amphibians, birds, etc.)
  • Daily updates via text or email
  • Water plants/garden
  • Will leave the house in same condition as you left it.
  • Administer medications, shots, etc.

Optional Services:

• Grooming (must provide supplies):

• $10 for small dogs
• $15 for medium dogs
• $25 for large dogs (Great Danes, Irish Wolf Hounds, Mastiffs, Newfoundlands)

• Training: Prices vary depending on success rate. If I’m only watching an animal for a few days, I won’t be able to make much progress, but I can train the owners of the animals to train effectively.

• House Cleaning (must provide supplies):  Prices vary depending on size of house and how dirty it is.

Additional Services:

  • Babysitting
  • House cleaning
  • Care giver
  • Special needs
