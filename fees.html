Fees

1 Dog: $40/day.

Extra $10/day for each additional dog.

Extra $5/day for each additional small animal.

Small animals (cats, rodents, reptiles, etc.): If I am staying at your home, $15/day minimum. Or $25/visit. Prices may vary depending on the situation.

Farm animals: negotiable prices.

To the owners:

  • I ask all owners to leave some cash or credit card in case of an emergency
  • Clear instructions must be typed and printed (or very neatly written) at time of arrival
  • Must include feeding instructions and times, usual walking times and routes, emergency contacts (vet name and number, a neighbor or someone that lives close who is familiar with the animal, and best way to contact you while you are away), treats, rules of the house (e.g. animals not allowed on furniture, etc.), and any other useful information.
  • Payment: cash, check, or Zelle, paid upfront
